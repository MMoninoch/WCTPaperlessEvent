Documentation

1. Registration Process

To register for our paperless event, navigate to the Registration page on our website. Follow the on-screen instructions to provide your details and complete the registration process.

2. Digital Tickets

Upon successful registration, you will receive a confirmation email containing your digital tickets. These tickets can be accessed through your email on your smartphone or printed for entry to the event.

3. Event App

Enhance your experience by downloading our dedicated event app. The app provides access to event schedules, maps, and real-time updates. Download it from the App Store or Google Play.

4. Making Changes to Registration

If you need to update your registration information, log in to your account on our website. Navigate to the Profile or Registration section to make any necessary changes.
