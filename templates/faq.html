Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for the event without using paper forms?

Registration for our event is entirely paperless. Simply visit our website, navigate to the registration page, and follow the easy online registration process.

Can I receive event updates and notifications digitally?

Absolutely! We provide real-time event updates, notifications, and any important announcements through our digital communication channels, such as email and SMS. Make sure to provide accurate contact information during the registration process.

You won't need to bring any physical documents. Upon successful registration, you'll receive a digital confirmation. Simply bring a digital or printed copy of the confirmation, and we'll take care of the rest!

How do I access my event tickets?

Your event tickets are digital! After completing the registration process, you'll receive an email containing your e-tickets. You can either show the digital ticket on your smartphone or bring a printed copy for entry.
